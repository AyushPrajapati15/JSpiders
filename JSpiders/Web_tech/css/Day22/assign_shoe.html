Air Jordan is a popular sneaker brand and model created by Michael Jordan, a famous basketball player. The first Air Jordan was released in 1985 by the Nike, Inc. Since then, the brand has produced several successful models, including the Air Jordan 2, Air Jordan 3, Air Jordan 4, and Air Jordan 5. These sneakers are known for their exceptional quality, comfort, and high performance, making them popular among athletes and fashion enthusiasts alike.
